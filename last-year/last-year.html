Block Parties Over the Years

       Every year, it's an honor to gather and celebrate the joy that our community brings. Here is a snapshot of some of the fun festivities over the years. This year we celebrate our 10th anniversary of the Maxwell Park Block Party. Together, we are commemorating the occassion by providing entertainment from some very special guests, including local magician, Brad Burner, and musical guest, Dua Lipa.
